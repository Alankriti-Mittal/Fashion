market segmentation : formal

Clothing

    A formal outfit for women typically includes a dress, a suit, or a skirt and blouse.
      The clothing should be well-fitted and made of high-quality fabric.
        Needs: Comfortable and stylish, Monochrome
          Examples: H&M, Forever 21, Zara

Footwear

    Women's formal shoes should be comfortable, elegant, and match the color of the outfit.
      Heels are often preferred, but flats are also acceptable.
        Needs: Elegant and high-quality
          Examples: Michael Kors, Tory Burch, Burberry

Accessories

    Accessories can make or break a formal look. Jewelry, such as earrings, necklaces, and bracelets, should be minimalistic and complement the outfit.
      A clutch or small handbag is also a good addition.
        A belt may also be necessary for a suit or dress.
          Examples: Nike, Adidas, Levi's

Hair and makeup

    Hair and makeup should be polished and well-groomed.
      A classic up-do or a sleek hairstyle is often preferred.
        Makeup should be subtle, with a focus on a natural look.
          Examples: Hugo Boss, Armani, Ralph Lauren

Outerwear

    A jacket or coat may be necessary, especially for colder weather.
      The outerwear should also match the outfit and be appropriate for the occasion.
        It adds a touch of completion to the look.
          Examples: The Children's Place, Carter's, OshKosh B'gosh
